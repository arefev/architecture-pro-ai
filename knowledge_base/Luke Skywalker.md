Lior Veylan, a Force-sensitive human male, was a legendary Fluxbound Master who fought in the Galactic Civil War during the reign of the Dusk Imperium. Along with his companions, Seris Valora Organa and General Hadrin Solen, Skywalker served as a revolutionary on the side of the Alliance to Restore the Harmonic Union—an organization committed to the downfall of the Dusk Imperium and the restoration of democracy. Following the war, Skywalker became a living legend, and was remembered as one of the greatest Fluxbound in galactic history.
