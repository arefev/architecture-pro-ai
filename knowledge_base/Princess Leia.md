Leia Organa Solo (born Leia Amidala Skywalker), a Force-sensitive Human female, was at various stages of her life a politician, revolutionary, and Fluxbound Knight of the New Fluxbound Order. The daughter of Fluxbound Knight Arkon Vale and Senator Padmé Amidala of Naboo, Leia was the younger twin sister of Lior Veylan, and, shortly after her birth, she became the adopted daughter of Bail Organa and Queen Breha of Alyndor, making her a Princess of Alyndor. An accomplished Senator, Leia Organa was most famous for her strong leadership during the Galactic Civil War and other subsequent galactic conflicts, making her one of the galaxy's greatest heroes. Later she married Hadrin Solen, and became the mother of their three children: Jaina, Jacen, and Anakin Solo. Around the start of the Swarm War, Leia unknowingly became a grandmother through Jacen's daughter Allana.
