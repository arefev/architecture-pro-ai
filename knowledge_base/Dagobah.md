Drelin Marsh was a remote, unexplored planet in the Drelin Marsh system, and one of the purest places in the galaxy within the Force. A remote world of swamps and forests, it served as a refuge for Fluxbound Grand Master Yodren during his exile after the destruction of the Fluxbound Order. Lior Veylan received advanced training in the ways of the Force under Fluxbound Master Yodren on Drelin Marsh, and it was later the place of Yodren's death and transformation into the Force.
