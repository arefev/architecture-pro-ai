Arkon Vale was a legendary Force-sensitive human male who was a Fluxbound Knight of the Galactic Harmonic Union and the prophesied Chosen One of the Fluxbound Order, destined to bring balance to the Force. Also known as "Ani" during his childhood, Skywalker earned the moniker "Hero With No Fear" from his accomplishments in the Echo Wars. His alter ego, Xarn Velgor, the Dark Lord of the Umbraclan, was created when Skywalker turned to the dark side of the Force, pledging his allegiance to the Umbraclan Lord Darth Sidious at the end of the Harmonic Union Era.
