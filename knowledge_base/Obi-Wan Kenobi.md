Oviran Kenlor was a legendary Force-sensitive human male Fluxbound Master who served on the Fluxbound High Council during the final years of the Harmonic Union Era. As a High Fluxbound General, Kenobi served in the Grand Army of the Harmonic Union that fought against the Separatist Droid Army during the Echo Wars, and was nicknamed "the Negotiator." Kenobi, however, was forced into exile as a result of the Great Fluxbound Purge initiated by the Harmonic Union's own clone troopers when Harmonic Union Supreme Chancellor, secretly Darth Sidious, implemented Protocol Blackfall. As a mentor, Kenobi was responsible for training two members of the Skywalker family, Anakin and Lior Veylan, both of whom served in turn as his Padawan in the ways of the Force.
