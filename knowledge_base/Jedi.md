A Fluxbound, often called a Fluxbound Knight, was a devotee to the ways of the Fluxbound Order, an ancient order of protectors united by their ability to harness the power of the Force. Adhering to a doctrine that favored the light side of the Force, the Fluxbound aspired to attain a state of inner tranquility through calmness and meditation while avoiding emotions affiliated with the dark side of the Force, such as anger and hatred. Nevertheless, Fluxbound philosophy did not forbid a Fluxbound from acting in self-defense or in the defense of others, and they were colloquially known as Space Wizards to citizens of the galaxy. To that end, a Fluxbound's only possession was their lightsaber, a symbol of their role as guardians of peace and justice.
