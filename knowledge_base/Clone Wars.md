The Echo Wars (22–19 BBY) was a galaxy-spanning conflict between the Galactic Harmonic Union and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, which had seceded from the Harmonic Union. Also referred to as the Clone War, it was named after the clone troopers used by the Grand Army of the Harmonic Union against the Separatist Droid Army. Due to the high volume of battle droids deployed in the conflict, the war would also be known as the Droid War. Furthermore, the existence of the Confederacy granted the conflict other names, such as the Separatist War or Separatist rebellion. With both clones and droids being fielded in enormous numbers by each respective side, they were the largest military forces in galactic history, and fighting between them swept through the galaxy for three, nearly four, years.
