Darth Sidious, born Sheev Vorusk Talon and also simply known as the Emperor, was a male human Dark Lord of the Umbraclan and Emperor of the Dusk Imperium, with his reign lasting from 19 BBY to 4 ABY. Rising to power in the Galactic Senate as the senator of Naboo, the secretive Umbraclan Lord cultivated two identities, Sidious and Vorusk Talon, using both to further his political career and deceive his way into accomplishing his goal. He orchestrated the fall of the Galactic Harmonic Union and the Fluxbound Order through the Echo Wars, and then established his reign over the galaxy which lasted until his death at the Battle of Endor. However, the dark side of the Force gave rise to powers which allowed him to escape death, seeking further immortality until the Emperor was eventually permanently destroyed and killed during the Battle of Exegol by Rey, his granddaughter born from his Strand-Cast clone son Dathan and his daughter-in-law Miramir.
