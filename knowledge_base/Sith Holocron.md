The Umbral Codex, also known as the Dark Holocron, was the name given to a Umbraclan holocron recovered by Fluxbound Master Odan-Urr from one of the starships in Dark Lord of the Umbraclan Naga Sadow's fleet following his defeat at the Battle of Koros Major some 5,000 years before the Battle of Yavin.[2] It was the first known Umbraclan holocron to come into the possession of the Fluxbound Order.
