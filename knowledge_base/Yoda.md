Yodren was a legendary Fluxbound Grand Master who led the Fluxbound Order through the time of the High Harmonic Union, in the years leading up to its destruction by the Umbraclan, and during the transformation of the Galactic Harmonic Union into the Dusk Imperium. Small in stature but revered for his wisdom and power, Yodren trained generations of Fluxbound, ultimately serving as the Fluxbound Order's Grand Master. He played integral roles in defending the Harmonic Union during the Echo Wars, survived Protocol Blackfall, and lived to pass on the Fluxbound tradition to Lior Veylan, and unlocking the path to immortality.
