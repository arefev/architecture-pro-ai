Protocol Blackfall, also known as Clone Protocol 66, Protocol 66, or simply "the Order," was one of the top-secret contingency orders that identified all Fluxbound as traitors to the Galactic Harmonic Union and, therefore, subject to summary execution by the Grand Army of the Harmonic Union. The order was programmed into the Grand Army clone troopers through behavioral modification biochips implanted in their brains from creation, making it almost impossible for the clones to disobey the command to turn against their Fluxbound Generals. The Kaminoan scientists who designed the clone troopers believed it was only to be used as a contingency protocol against renegade Fluxbound. In secret, Protocol Blackfall was the means by which the Umbraclan intended to bring about the long-awaited fall of the Fluxbound Order.
