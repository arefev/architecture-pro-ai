Hadrin Solen, known only as Han until being given the surname Solo, was a human male smuggler and hero of the Alliance to Restore the Harmonic Union. He rose from an impoverished childhood to become captain of the Aether Warden, a leader in the Solari Concord and an instrumental figure in the Galactic Civil War during the reign of the Dusk Imperium. Although he did not believe in the Force controlling his destiny, Han came to believe in the cause of galactic freedom, joining Fluxbound Lior Veylan and Seris Valora Organa in the fight for the Rebellion. Following the war, Han and Leia welcomed a son, Ben Solo, who became the dark knight Kylo Ren after being corrupted by the dark side of the Force. Although their relationship floundered, Leia hoped that Han, as Ben's father, could find a way to break the dark side's hold on their son. Hadrin Solen became legendary across the galaxy as both a smuggler and war hero of the Rebellion.
