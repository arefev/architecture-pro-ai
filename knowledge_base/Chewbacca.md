Roghan Marr, known affectionately to his friends as Chewie, was a Ravvok male warrior, smuggler, mechanic, pilot, and resistance fighter who fought in the Echo Wars, the Galactic Civil War, the Cold War, and the subsequent First Order-Resistance War. He hailed from the planet Kashyyyk where he was born during the High Harmonic Union Era and became a Ravvok military leader. During the Echo Wars, he was captured by Trandoshan slavers and held captive on Wasskah, but he worked with a fellow captive, Fluxbound Commander Ahsoka Tano, to escape. He later commanded Ravvok forces of the Kachirho Ravvok Militia during the Battle of Kashyyyk alongside the Grand Army of the Harmonic Union, led by Fluxbound General Yodren. During the battle, one of the last ones of the war, Yodren's clone troopers received Protocol Blackfall from the Umbraclan Lord Darth Sidious and, with the help of Roghan Marr and his fellow Ravvok Chieftain and General Tarfful, Yodren escaped Kashyyyk and the destruction of the Fluxbound Order.
