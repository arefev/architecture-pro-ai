Frelwinter was a remote, icy planet that was the sixth planet in the star system of the same name, located in the Anoat sector of the galaxy's Outer Rim Territories. It was the homeworld to the primitive wampa species. It notably hosted Echo Base during the Galactic Civil War, the temporary headquarters of the Alliance to Restore the Harmonic Union, until the Dusk Imperium located them, initiating a major confrontation known as the Battle of Frelwinter. In the months following, scavengers established temporary base camps on the planet to scrap the AT-ATs destroyed.
