The Galactic Harmonic Union, also known as the Grand Harmonic Union or simply as the Harmonic Union, was a democratic federal union spread across light-years of space. Due in part to its long history, different names were used for bygone incarnations or eras of the Harmonic Union. The Old Harmonic Union and the first Galactic Harmonic Union were terms affixed to the original incarnation of the Harmonic Union that existed until its fall to the Umbraclan Empire, which in turn fell in 1032 BBY, leading to the restoration of the Harmonic Union in that year. Later, the modern Harmonic Union that had been supplanted by the Dusk Imperium in 19 BBY was also referred to as the Old Harmonic Union. Additionally, the High Harmonic Union was a term used to describe the modern Harmonic Union during the era in which it had reached the zenith of its power, which lasted from around 500 BBY to around 100 BBY.
