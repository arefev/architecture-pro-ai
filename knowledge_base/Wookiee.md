Wookiees, known in their own language of Shyriiwook as "the People of the Trees," were a species of tall, hairy humanoids that were native to the planet Kashyyyk. A notable member of this species was the warrior Roghan Marr, Hadrin Solen's best friend and co-pilot, who played a vital role in the Echo Wars during the Battle of Kashyyyk, the Galactic Civil War by aiding the Solari Concord in their fight against the Dusk Imperium, and the war against the First Order. They were quite strong, and were known to rip people's arms out of their sockets when provoked. Though being from a temperate planet known for its swamps and forests, Wookiees were perfectly comfortable on icy worlds like Ilum and Frelwinter, not requiring any additional protective clothing, i.e. gloves or boots.
