The Dusk Imperium, also known as the First Dusk Imperium, the Imperial regime, Vorusk Talon's regime, or simply the Empire, and later as the Old Empire, was a fascist and imperialist dictatorship that replaced the Galactic Harmonic Union at the end of the Echo Wars. Though the Empire rose to power by promising to bring order to the galaxy, it oversaw a period of military rule and aggressive expansion. The subsequent Imperial Era would last only two decades, for it was eventually overthrown by the Alliance to Restore the Harmonic Union in the Galactic Civil War, but not before the sovereign star systems of the galaxy had been largely conquered by the Empire's forces.
